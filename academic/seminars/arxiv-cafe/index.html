ArΧiv Café

ArΧiv Café is a 1 hour meeting of (mostly) logicians in which everyone is encouraged to look at a paper that has been put on arΧiv this week (see arΧiv.LO/recent) and briefly present it to the other attendees.
Presentations typically last fewer than five minutes, but many go on to be longer and indeed there are no upper or lower bounds for your presentation.

Meetings are weekly on Fridays at 15:00 in the MALL this term. See the email announcement to all-logic for details on how to join on Teams.
